Rules & Guidlines


بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.




By the will of Allah, our goal is to help each other and have a good time in Muslim Gamer’s League. However,
in order to ensure the best experience for everybody at MGL, you MUST read and comply with MGL’s rules and
regulations. This may be a very long list, but the MGL Management Team’s first priority is to ensure that all of
our Muslim brothers and sisters have a good time together in a safe and halal environment, Inshallah.

  • Do NOT break https://discordapp.com/terms.

  • Do NOT break https://discordapp.com/guidelines.

Any pornographic, erotic, or sexually explicit posts will NOT be tolerated. Absolutely NO profanity/foul language. Don’t post anything that isn’t safe for work. No racism, sexism, or any posts related to such matters. discuss anything sensitive, suggestive, or “edgy.” Don’t discuss politics, drugs, violence, or any other controversial matter. :Don’t impersonate anybody. If we suspect you are an imposter, you will receive an instant ban without any warning or strike. NO TROLLING. If we are suspicious that you are a troll, the MGL Management Team reserves the right to issue a strike or an instant kick/ban. Do NOT mock Islam or spread false ideology, such as anything related to Islamophobia or anything else haram.


Everyone, violation of these “ground rules” will result in an instant kick/ban depending on the severity. NO EXCEPTIONS.

Keep profile pictures appropriate. Statuses should be free of profanity and/or foul language. Names should be appropriate and contain some standard characters so they are easily taggable with an English keyboard.You may receive a kick or a ban if violation of this rule is severe or extreme. Respect your fellow Muslim brothers, sisters, and the MGL Management Team. NO GOSSIP. It is a very evil sin and will hurt you on the Day of Judgement. NO SPAMMING! NO DISCUSSIONS regarding games and fatwah. If you believe a game is haram, just don't talk about it. Games are games, not real life; you can take it easy. If a game is absolutely haram, then don't talk about it. The "common sense" rule applies here! NO DISCUSSIONS about scholars and their trustworthiness. If you disagree with someone about certain scholars, or you despise a post that any person made in the #📺┋islamic-media, that is solely your opinion. Discussing such matters, whether in this server and/or in a DM with a Moderator, Administrator, or the Owner, is prohibited. Attempting to advertise products or trying to scam others will result in an instant strike without a warning. Don’t ping @everyone, or spam ping. The MGL Management Team reserves the right to ping anybody, including @everyone, as needed. If you are getting spam pinged, or you would like not to be pinged, let us know in a DM. Read the channel descriptions CAREFULLY and do not break any extra rules listed in the channel descriptions. Stay on channel topic.


Violation of these “general rules” will result in a strike. 3 strikes and you are out!

We welcome suggestions for MGL. However, we reserve the right to deny any suggestions for channels linked to haram. If you have been stricken, kicked, or banned, and you believe this is a mistake, you may DM one of our team members for an appeal in a respectful manner. The MGL Management Team (consists of @Level 1┋Moderator, @King┋Moderators, @⚔️┋Admins, and @👑┋Owner) has the final say in any matter or dispute. Let's maintain positive vibes and avoid conversations related to negative, evil, scary thoughts/matters. We are here to have fun! The MGL Management Team reserves the right to intervene in a conversation when needed. If you fight back with an MGL Management Team member, whether in this server or in a DM, you will be banned and blocked. You have been officially warned. Rules are subject to common sense. If a basic rule is implied but not explicitly stated, please do not try to loophole around it; also do not loophole around our other rules! Glorify Allah (SWT) and praise His Messenger (PBUH) when mentioned in a conversation. Greet each other with the salutations of peace. Please practice good Islamic etiquette. Don’t break any Islamic rules. Brothers and sisters, kindly avoid intermingling, please! The rules are subject to change. @everyone, it is YOUR responsibility to frequently check back on them to stay updated. If you break a rule that you never noticed was there, that's on you. Don't say we didn't warn you!


@everyone If we follow these rules, Inshallah, we will have an epic time together in Muslim Gamer’s League. Allah will bless us and put barakah on this server. Let’s try to make that happen If you have any questions, do NOT hesitate to DM or speak to the @Level 1┋Moderator, @King┋Moderators, @⚔️┋Admins, and/or the @👑┋Owner (aka Br. Yusha himself).
