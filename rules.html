Rules & Guidlines


بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.



By the will of Allah, our goal is to help each other and have a good time in Muslim Gamer’s League. However,
in order to ensure the best experience for everybody at MGL, you MUST read and comply with MGL’s rules and
regulations. This may be a very long list, but the MGL Management Team’s first priority is to ensure that all of
our Muslim brothers and sisters have a good time together in a safe and halal environment, Inshallah.


By verifying yourself as a member, you hereby agree to adhere to this whole page. You also understand,
are aware of, and are willing to accept that you may be pinged frequently
as needed in this server. You have been officially alerted✅
Please ping or DM a Staff Member if you have any questions.

  • Do NOT break https://discordapp.com/terms.

  • Do NOT break https://discordapp.com/guidelines.

1. No posts that aren’t safe for work.
2. No trolling or impersonating.
3. No mocking Islam or spreading false information.
4. No profanity or foul language.
5. Don’t discuss sex, drugs, violence, politics, controversial matters, or anything of the like.
6. No racism, sexism, heterosexism, supremacism, extremism, nationalism, or anything of the like.
7. All rules are subject to common sense. No loopholes allowed. Keep in mind that any implied rules that are not explicitly stated are still under effect.
8. Be respectful. No judgements or harsh comments.
9. Names, profile pictures, and statuses should be appropriate and adhere to this whole list.
10. No negative vibes or gossip. Let’s stay positive.
11. Do not spam, copypasta, or flood.
12. No pinging @ everyone.
13. Stay on channel topic. Scroll through all the channels so you know where to post something. Repetitive offenses are subject to punishment.
14. No discussions about games and fatwah. If you object games in Islam, feel free to leave.
15. No scamming, advertising products, or buying/selling in this server.

1. Each of the designated game channels are for discussing the game or for party scouting.
2. For party scouting, feel free to ping the designated role if you wish. Do not abuse this privilege.
3. No trash talking.

1. Upon breaking a rule, you will receive a verbal warning. Continuing such behaviors will result in a permanent strike and you will be muted for a period of time.
2. 3 strikes and you will immediately be dismissed without exception.
3. Instant kicks/bans are imminent depending on the severity of violation.
4. All rules and punishments carry over to DMs with Staff members.
5. This whole page is subject to change with tweaks and amendments anytime without announcement. It is each and every member's responsibility to check back frequently so they stay updated with the rules. If you break a rule without knowing that it has been recently added, you are still subject to punishment. No exceptions.

1. Upon a punishment, you can DM a Staff member for an appeal unless otherwise stated.
2. If your request for appeal gets accepted, answer the following questions:
--> What did you do wrong? Admit yourself, don't make excuses.
--> How will you improve yourself?
--> Why do you want your punishment revoked? Why should we do so? Convince us and you may get a second chance. Be honest.
3. If you choose to break more rules after your appeal has been accepted, the punishment severity will multiply.
4. Be warned: you are only allowed one appeal to be accepted. Afterwards, no further appeals will be accepted.
